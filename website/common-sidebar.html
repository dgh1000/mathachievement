I offer private tutoring sessions in high school and college math, including algebra, geometry, trigonometry, pre-calculus, and calculus. My goal is to promote a deeper understanding of math problem solving, equipping my students with tools to help them in both their current math classes and future math classes. In the Pasadena, California area I will come to your home. In other areas, I can provide online sessions. Contact Mike for more information.
